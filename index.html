Decentralized & Secure Medical Record Management

AptoMed leverages the Aptos blockchain to ensure privacy, data integrity, and secure, controlled access to medical records globally.

Key Features

Decentralized Storage

AptoMed securely stores medical records on the Aptos blockchain, ensuring privacy and data integrity.

Smart Contract-Based Access Control

Patients use smart contracts to grant timed access to healthcare providers for secure medical data sharing.

Cross-Border Medical Consultation

Enables secure sharing of medical records globally without compromising patient privacy.
